Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Bark & Wiggle, we strive to provide our clients with the best possible service. If you have any questions or concerns about your booking, please contact our customer service team.

Cancellation Policy

  • If the booking is cancelled within 24 hours of the service date, the full payment will be refunded.
  • If the booking is cancelled within 24-48 hours of the service date, 50% of the payment will be refunded.
  • If the booking is cancelled within 48 hours of the service date, no payment will be refunded.

Refund Policy

  • Payments will be refunded through the original payment method.
  • Payments will be refunded within 7-10 days after the service date.

Terms and Conditions

  • Payments will not be refunded if the booking is cancelled due to reasons beyond Bark & Wiggle's control, such as natural disasters or changes in government policies.
  • Payments will not be refunded if the booking is cancelled due to reasons beyond the client's control, such as personal problems or changes in plans.
