About Bark & Wiggle

Bark & Wiggle Pet Grooming & Store started as a humble coffee shop in Caloocan City. After seeing the changing needs of the community and the arrival of new local businesses, the owners made a heartfelt decision to shift into something that would bring joy to both pets and their owners.

Our store now specializes in pet grooming services and pet care essentials. We believe that every pet deserves to be pampered and loved, and every owner deserves convenience and trust in the services they choose. At Bark & Wiggle, your furry friend is treated like family.

Our Values

  • Compassionate care for all pets
  • Customer satisfaction and comfort
  • Reliable and professional grooming services
  • Affordable, high-quality pet supplies

Thank you for supporting local. We look forward to meeting your pets and helping them bark and wiggle their way to happiness!
